How It Works

Three simple steps.
Infinite community power.

CommVault distils trust into code. No middlemen, no broken promises — just transparent rules enforced on-chain.

01
Connect Your Wallet
Link your Lace or compatible Cardano wallet with a single click. No registration, no email, no KYC — your wallet is your identity.
02
Deposit to the Pool
Any community member can deposit ADA directly into the shared smart contract. Funds are locked transparently on-chain — visible to everyone, controlled by no single person.
03
Treasurer Withdraws
Only the pre-assigned treasurer wallet can withdraw funds. The Plutus V2 smart contract enforces this cryptographically — no one else can access pool funds, ever.
Features

Everything your community needs

Built specifically for group savings — ROSCAs, susus, ajo, tontines — upgraded with the security of blockchain.

Treasurer-Controlled Withdrawals
A single designated wallet holds withdrawal authority, encoded in the Plutus V2 smart contract as an inline datum. No multi-sig complexity — just clear, enforceable community rules.
PlutusV2Inline Datum
Open Multi-Member Deposits
Any number of community members can deposit ADA into the shared pool contract. No permission required — just a connected wallet and the desire to save together.
Open AccessMulti-User
Fully On-Chain Transparency
Every deposit and withdrawal is recorded on the Cardano blockchain. The pool balance is publicly verifiable by anyone — complete financial transparency with no off-chain records needed.
ImmutablePublic
Partial Withdrawals Supported
The treasurer can withdraw any amount up to the pool balance. Remaining funds stay locked in the contract with the datum preserved — the pool continues without interruption.
FlexibleNon-destructive
Smart Contract Security
The withdrawal logic is enforced by a Plutus V2 validator script. The treasurer's public key hash is embedded in the datum — no one can alter it, and no platform can override it.
TrustlessImmutable Logic
Zero Platform Custody
CommVault never holds your funds. The smart contract address is the pool — not a company wallet, not a custodian. Your community's savings are always under on-chain rules, not corporate policies.

Why CommVault

Traditional savings culture meets blockchain security

Communities have practised collective savings for generations — ROSCAs, susus, ajo, tontines. CommVault brings these traditions on-chain, replacing social trust with mathematical certainty.

No Trust Required
The smart contract enforces the rules. The treasurer cannot take more than designated. Depositors cannot withdraw. The code is the agreement.
Instant Settlement
All transactions settle directly on the Cardano blockchain in seconds. No waiting, no bank clearance, no remittance delays — funds move at blockchain speed.
Complete Auditability
Every ADA that enters or leaves the pool is recorded permanently on-chain. Any member — or auditor — can verify the pool's history at any time, forever.
Works With Your Wallet
CommVault connects to Lace and compatible Cardano wallets. No new accounts, no custody — your keys, your funds, your community.
