How It Works
The Collective Journey
Streamlined, transparent fund management for decentralised communities.
01
Deposit
Any community member can contribute ADA instantly via audited Plutus smart contracts. No minimums, no gatekeepers — just trustless participation.
02
Secure
Funds are pooled and secured by Cardano's Proof of Stake consensus. Your assets are protected by mathematics, not intermediaries.
03
Propose & Disburse
Only the designated treasurer's signing key may authorise withdrawals. Submit a governed proposal and the community traces every disbursement on-chain.
